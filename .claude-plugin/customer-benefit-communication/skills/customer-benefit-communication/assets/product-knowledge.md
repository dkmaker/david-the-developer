# Product Knowledge — Benefit Framing Guide

Use this as a starting point for common product categories. **Always web search
to verify** current features, pricing, and availability before making specific
claims in customer communication. Products and plans change frequently.

> 💡 **Tip:** Replace or extend this file with your company's actual product stack
> and approved benefit statements. The more specific to your offering, the better.

## When to Research

Trigger product research when communication involves:
- Recommending or explaining a specific product or feature
- Comparing plans or justifying an upgrade
- Responding to customer questions about capabilities
- Writing proposals or onboarding material
- Mentioning pricing, licensing, or what's included

## Research Checklist

Before including product-specific claims in any communication:

- [ ] Is this feature still current? (web search to verify)
- [ ] Is pricing accurate? (changes frequently — search or omit)
- [ ] Is the feature included in the customer's actual plan?
- [ ] Am I explaining the benefit, not just the feature?
- [ ] Would the customer understand this without IT knowledge?
