# Step 1: Detect Communication Type & Recipient

## Channel Detection

| Signal | Characteristics | Adapt |
|--------|----------------|-------|
| **Email / chat** | Short, direct | 2-5 benefit sentences per topic |
| **Status report** | Structured, recurring | Benefit summary per line item |
| **Proposal / quote** | Persuasive, detailed | Benefit-led sections |
| **Support reply** | Quick, reassuring | One benefit statement |
| **Presentation** | Visual, high-level | Headline benefits with narrative |
| **Newsletter** | Informative, broad | Curated benefit highlights |

## Recipient Role

| Role | What they care about | Tone |
|------|---------------------|------|
| **CEO / Owner** | ROI, growth, risk, competitive edge | Strategic, concise |
| **Office manager / admin** | Day-to-day ease, fewer headaches | Warm, practical |
| **IT responsible** | Stability, compliance, control | Professional, some technical OK |
| **Finance responsible** | Cost, budget, predictability | Numbers-driven |
| **End user** | "It just works" | Simple, friendly |

## Lifecycle Stage

| Stage | Communication focus |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Prospect** | What you'll gain by choosing us |
| **Onboarding** | What's happening and why it benefits you |
| **Active customer** | Ongoing value you're receiving |
| **Renewal / upsell** | Additional value available to you |
