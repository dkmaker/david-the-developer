Extended examples for each communication channel. Load this file when you need
more examples beyond what's in SKILL.md.

## Email — Status Update

### Before
```
Hi [customer],

Over the weekend we completed the following:
- Updated Exchange Online to the latest version
- Enabled Conditional Access policies
- Migrated file server to SharePoint Online
- Installed new endpoint protection agents

Regards
[Company] Support
```

### After
```
Hi [customer],

Over the weekend we made three improvements that strengthen your everyday work:

1. **Your email is now even more stable** — we updated the mail system so you
   experience fewer interruptions during the day.

2. **Security has been tightened** — your systems now automatically verify that
   the right people are logging in from approved devices. This protects you
   against unauthorised access.

3. **Your files are now in the cloud** — everyone can securely access files from
   the office, from home, or on the go. Everything is automatically backed up,
   so you'll never lose an important document.

You don't need to do anything — everything works as before, just better and more secure.

Have a great week!
[Company]
```

```
- 3 critical incidents handled

```

```
## IT Overview — March 2026

**Your operations ran smoothly:** Your systems were available 99.95% of the time
this month — that's less than 22 minutes of total downtime.

**We were there when you needed us:** We resolved 52 requests from your team,

**Proactive protection:** We handled 3 potential issues before they impacted
your work. 98% of all your devices are up to date with the latest security patches.

**Your team is growing:** We onboarded 2 new employees who were ready to work
from day one — with access to all systems from the start.
```
